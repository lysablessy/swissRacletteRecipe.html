I discovered this classic Swiss raclette recipe after arriving in Switzerland. It's a simple, cozy meal that brings people together.

Ingredients

  • Raclette cheese (200 g per person)
  • Small potatoes
  • Pickles (cornichons)
  • Pickled onions
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil potatoes until tender.
  2. Heat the raclette cheese.
  3. Pour cheese over potatoes.
  4. Add pickles and pepper.
  5. Enjoy immediately.
